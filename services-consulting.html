Research Consulting: Turn Your Thesis Into a Project

Guiding university students to achieve academic success through structured, project-based thesis planning.

Introduction

Writing a thesis is a complex, time-intensive challenge, especially for those studying in a foreign country. From adapting to different academic principles to balancing cultural nuances, international students face unique hurdles. I offer structured, project-oriented workshops as well as individual consulting designed to demystify the thesis process and provide students with the skills they need to plan, write, and complete their thesis with confidence.

Supporting Your Students’ Success

The workshops are specifically designed to meet the diverse needs of students in international settings. Conducted primarily in English, these courses are well-suited for international students, yet they can also be adapted for Japanese students or offered entirely in Japanese upon request. With a solid background in multicultural higher education, I bring insights into the varied academic expectations across cultures and tailor my approach to support students effectively, no matter their background.

Workshop Structure and Customization

My half-day workshops are offered online for flexible access or in person, if preferred. For on-site workshops, the commissioning institution is asked to provide the facilities. Each workshop generally accommodates up to 20 students. I am, however, open to customizing the format to meet the specific needs of your institution and student population.

Following the workshop, I offer one-on-one follow-up sessions for students who seek additional guidance on individual challenges or detailed feedback.

Key Benefits for Your Students

  • Structured Guidance: My workshops simplify the thesis process into manageable, actionable stages, providing students with clear strategies and practical tools.
  • Cross-Cultural Understanding: I bring valuable insights that address the distinct challenges international students may face when adapting to new academic environments.
  • Individualized Support: For students who need additional guidance, one-on-one sessions offer tailored advice to tackle personal hurdles and keep them on track.
